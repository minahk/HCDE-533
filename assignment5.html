Subtractive Manufacturing

Part 1: Modifying meshes

Steps:

1. Imported two STL files from Thingverse, the bowl was a closed polysurface and the cup was an open polysurface.

2. I used the "ReduceMesh" command to reduce the size of the mesh of the cup by 85%. This turned the cup into a closed polysurface.

3. I placed the cup on top of the bowl and used "MeshBooleanUnion" to join them together as one object.
